    • Cyber Security


    Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, and damage. It uses technologies and processes to safeguard sensitive information and maintain data integrity. With rising cyber threats like malware and phishing, cybersecurity defends against breaches that can cause financial loss and privacy violations, ensuring critical systems remain secure.


    What you will learn


    Network Security

    Understanding how to protect networks from unauthorized access, misuse, and attacks through firewalls, VPNs, and intrusion detection systems.


    Threat Analysis and Risk Management

    Identifying, analyzing, and managing potential threats and vulnerabilities to minimize risks to systems and data.


    Cryptography

    Learning encryption techniques to secure data in transit and storage, ensuring confidentiality and integrity.


    Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing

    Gaining skills in simulating cyber attacks to find and fix vulnerabilities before malicious hackers can exploit them.


    Incident Response and Recovery

    Developing strategies to detect, respond to, and recover from cybersecurity incidents like data breaches and malware infections.


    Security Compliance and Governance

    Understanding legal and regulatory standards, such as GDPR and ISO, to ensure organizational adherence to cybersecurity policies.
